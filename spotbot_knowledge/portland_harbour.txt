Spot Name: Portland Harbour (Weymouth Harbour)

The Vibe & Disciplines

    Disciplines: Slalom, Freestyle, Speed, IQ Foil, Bump and Jump (in Easterlies).

    Skill Level: All abilities; highly safe enclosed area.

    Legacy: Premier speed sailing spot; home to world records and Weymouth Speed Week.

Conditions & Wind Directions

    Ideal Wind: SW and W (prevailing).

    Wind Character: W and SW provide super flat water behind Chesil Beach bank. N and S directions can be gusty.

    Tides: Accessible at all tides, but low spring tides create a very long walk to reach deep water.

    Tide Quirks: Features a unique "double low tide" (water goes out, comes in slightly, then goes out again).

Launch Sites, Parking & Facilities

    Ferrybridge Boat Yard Car Park: Most convenient; park virtually on the beach (avoiding road crossing). Includes toilets, cafe, and likely showers/changing rooms. Note: Can be fairly expensive.

    Chesil Beach (Council) Car Park: Larger and less expensive with stacks of rigging space on grass. Warning: Requires a long walk and crossing the busy main A354 road with kit.

    WPNSA (Osprey Quay) / OTC: State-of-the-art academy. Includes carpeted rigging, showers, and on-site pro shop (Windtek nearby).

    Hamm Beach: Launching near Hamm Beach roundabout ("Windy Corner").

Hazards & Safety

    Sandbanks: At low spring tides, water depth disappears fast. Danger of hitting fins/sand at high speed.

    Currents: Strong ebbing tide flow under Ferrybridge.

    Exclusion Zone: (June–Sept) Seasonal buoy line off Hamm Beach/Billy Winter’s is impassable.

    Shipping: Large warships and active boat traffic.

    Kit: Carry a knife and spare rope due to strong currents.

Local Amenities & Gear

    Accommodation: Plenty of B&Bs in Wyke Regis/Portland; camping and caravans nearby.

    Shops: Windtek shop located on the A354 approach.

    Permits: Harbour Dues are mandatory and payable online or at agents.
