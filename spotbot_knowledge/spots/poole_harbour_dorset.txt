**Windsurf Guide for Poole Harbour, Dorset**

**Introduction**

Poole Harbour in Dorset is a popular destination for windsurfers of all levels, offering a wide range of conditions and a safe and protected environment. The harbour is home to a dedicated board sailing area in Whitley Lake at Sandbanks, which is shallow and safe, making it ideal for novices.

**Best Time to Windsurf**

The best time to windsurf in Poole Harbour is during the summer months when the wind is moderate and the conditions are calm. However, the harbour is suitable for windsurfing throughout the year, with consistent wind conditions all year round.

**Launch and Landing Spots**

The dedicated board sailing area in Whitley Lake at Sandbanks is the best spot to launch and land your windsurfing equipment. The area is shallow and safe, making it ideal for novices and intermediate windsurfers.

**Wind Conditions**

The wind conditions in Poole Harbour are generally moderate, with a mix of onshore and offshore winds. The harbour is protected from the open sea, making it a great spot for windsurfers of all levels.

**Safety Precautions**

When windsurfing in Poole Harbour, it's essential to be aware of the following safety precautions:

* Always wear a life jacket and helmet
* Be aware of other water users, including sailors, motorboats, and swimmers
* Keep a safe distance from the harbour entrance and shipping channels
* Be mindful of the chain ferry in the entrance to the harbour and give way to it at all times
* Use a motor if fitted when transiting the harbour entrance

**Regulations**

Windsurfing is allowed in Poole Harbour, but there are some regulations to be aware of:

* A permit is required for kitesurfing inside the harbour
* Windsurfing is not allowed in the harbour entrance or shipping channels
* There are designated quiet areas and speed limits in place to protect the environment and other water users

**Marinas and Facilities**

Poole Harbour has several marinas and facilities that offer a range of services, including:

* Salterns Marina: offers permanent and visitor berths, swinging moorings, and storage ashore
* Lake Yard Marina: offers a range of facilities, including a chandlery and brokerage sales office
