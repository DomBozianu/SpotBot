**Gott Bay, Tiree Windsurfing Guide**

**Overview**

Gott Bay is a world-renowned windsurfing spot located on the island of Tiree, Scotland. The bay's long beach and large shallow-water area make it a favorite among beginners, freestyle, and freeride enthusiasts. The spot offers gentle waves in the eastern corner, making it an ideal location for all levels of windsurfers.

**Features**

* Long beach with a large shallow-water area (400x1,000m)
* Gentle waves in the eastern corner
* Suitable for beginners, freestyle, and freeride enthusiasts
* Accessible parking and close proximity to the Tiree Lodge hotel
* Good water quality and a sandy bottom

**Hazards**

* Rocks to the right of the bay looking out to sea and small clumps of rocks in the middle of the beach
* Strong tidal currents at the entrance of the bay
* Very cold water (5/4 wetsuit minimum)
* Wind can pick up quickly on this exposed island

**Tide Information**

* Suggested Tide ID: 5141 (based on the nearest port, Tiree)
* Tide range: large
* Shelving rate: shallow shelving

**Wind and Wave Conditions**

* Best wind direction: South
* Worst wind direction: North-west
* Wave state: The beauty of Tiree is that it can provide bolt flat water and down-the-line sailing at the same time – you just have to pick your beach.

**Instruction and Kit Hire**

* Equipment hire and tuition for all levels are available from Wild Diamond Windsurfing
* Wild Diamond also has a shop based behind the Tiree Lodge hotel at Gott Bay, offering a full mail order service and carrying a range of spares, hardware, wetsuits, and other neoprene supplies.

**Getting There**

* By Cal Mac Ferries from Oban (around 4 hours sailing time)
* Parking: Not an issue
* Accommodation: In Balemartine

**Amenities**

* Water quality: Not tested
* Suitable for: All abilities
* Our rating: 5/5 stars

**Tips and Recommendations**

* An advanced level of sailing is recommended before taking the reefs on!
* The Loch (Bhasapol) is home to Wild Diamond Windsurfing, an RYA Level 5 recognized center, and offers a large area of shallow water perfect for learning.
* The Loch is private, and a minimal launch fee is payable to Tiree Windsurfing Club.
* The spot is suitable for all levels, from beginners to expert windsurfers.
