**Camber Sands Windsurfing Guide**

**Location:** Camber Sands, Camber Sands, Sussex

**Suggested Tide ID:** 4-digit tide ID for Camber Harbour (nearest port)

**Suitability:** Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced

**Beach Faces:** S

**Best Wind:** E, W, SW

**Tidal Range:** Medium

**Shelving Rate:** Shingle banks at Broomhill

**Windsurfing Ratings:**

* Wave: [insert rating]
* Freestyle: [insert rating]
* Speed: [insert rating]
* Beginner: [insert rating]

**Features:**

* Shallow shelving sands make for a good location for learning
* Consistent onshore breezes throughout the season
* Shallow water due to a tidal range of about a mile

**Considerations:**

* Be aware of tidal movements, as the tide can come in quickly and catch people off guard on the sandbars
* Never turn your back on an incoming tide
* Strong currents exist near the harbour entrance at the western end, stay well clear of this area
* Always wear proper swimwear, as wet normal clothes can become heavy and drag you under

**Hazards:**

* Kitesurfers and kiters in the area, be aware and keep out of their way
* Old shipwreck downwind that can be hidden by waves at high tide
* Groynes at Jury's at high tide

**Facilities:**

* Public toilets in the main car parks near the beach, regularly cleaned
* Outside taps for rinsing off sandy feet
* The Kitesurf Centre, Camber Sands, offers lessons and equipment hire for kite-surfing, kite buggying, kite landboarding, windsurfing, and stand-up paddleboarding

**Safety:**

* RNLI lifeguards patrol Camber Sands during summer, typically late May to early September, 10am to 6pm daily
* Red/yellow flags indicate safe swimming and bodyboarding areas
* Black/white chequered flags indicate surf craft and kayaks
* Red flag indicates danger, do not enter the water
* Orange windsock indicates offshore winds, no inflatables
* Lost children wristbands are available from lifeguards and the coastal office

**Emergency Contacts:**

* In an emergency, call 999 and ask for the Coastguard
* Coastal Officer: 01797 225207 (8am to 5pm)
* Mobile: 07714 696218
* Email: camberoffice@rother.gov.uk
* Nearest hospital: Conquest Hospital, Hastings (30 min drive)
