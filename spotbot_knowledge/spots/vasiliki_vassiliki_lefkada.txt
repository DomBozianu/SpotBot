**Vasiliki Windsurf Guide**

**Location:** Vasiliki, Vassiliki, Lefkada, Greece

**Suggested Tide ID:** 4124 (based on the nearest port, Vasiliki)

**Wind Conditions:**

Vasiliki is renowned for its consistent and reliable winds, making it a paradise for windsurfers. The wind, known as "Eric," blows steadily from April to October, with the strongest winds typically occurring in the afternoon. The average wind speed is around 15-20 knots, with occasional gusts reaching up to 30 knots.

**Best Time to Visit:**

The windsurfing season in Vasiliki typically starts in April and ends in October, with July and August being the peak months. The wind is usually at its strongest in the afternoon, making it ideal for windsurfers.

**Beach and Water Conditions:**

The beach in Vasiliki is a large, pebble beach that is perfect for windsurfing. The water is crystal-clear and shallow, making it ideal for beginners. The wind direction is generally from the right, making it easy to launch and land your board.

**Windsurfing Spots:**

Vasiliki has several windsurfing spots, including:

* The main beach: This is the most popular spot for windsurfing, with a large, flat area perfect for launching and landing your board.
* The north beach: This spot is ideal for beginners, with a smaller, more sheltered area.
* The south beach: This spot is perfect for experienced windsurfers, with a larger, more challenging area.

**Windsurfing Schools and Rentals:**

There are several windsurfing schools and rental centers in Vasiliki, offering a range of services, including lessons, rentals, and repairs. Some of the most popular schools include:

* Club Vass
* Windsurfing Club Grand Nefeli
* Vassiliki Windsurfing School

**Tips and Essentials:**

* Make sure to check the wind forecast before heading out.
* Wear a wetsuit, as the water temperature can be cool, even in the summer months.
* Bring sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses to protect yourself from the sun.
* Respect the local environment and other beach users.
* Don't forget to try some of the local cuisine and drinks, such as Greek salad, moussaka, and ouzo!

**Conclusion:**

Vasiliki is a windsurfing paradise, with its consistent and reliable winds, crystal-clear water, and stunning scenery. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced windsurfer, Vasiliki has something to offer. So why not come and experience it for yourself?
