**Troon South Beach Windsurf Guide**

Located in Ayrshire, Scotland, Troon South Beach is a popular spot for windsurfers and kitesurfers. The beach offers a vast and sandy area with plenty of room for everyone, a good friendly vibe, and excellent sailing conditions.

**Getting There**

To get to Troon South Beach, take the A77 from Glasgow and follow the signs for Prestwick. Turn onto the A719 and take the first right across the dual carriageway to the Links Course car park. From there, it's a short walk to the beach.

**Parking**

There is a free public car park at the beach, located halfway down the beach. Be aware that it can get busy at weekends during spring and summer, so it's best to arrive early.

**Tides**

Troon South Beach is suitable for all tides. However, be aware that if the wind is strong and the tide is high, there may only be a small area of beach left to launch and land.

**Wind**

The ideal wind direction for Troon South Beach is anything with a south component (S). This will give you cross-off to cross-on conditions.

**Wave Power**

The wave power at Troon South Beach is low, but the wave size can reach up to 2-3 meters on the outer rocky reefs. The reefs to watch are directly off the beach and at right angles to it, as well as one due south.

**Best Conditions**

The best conditions for waves are at medium to low tide, when the outer reefs are exposed. In summer, at higher points of the tide, Troon South Beach offers excellent sailing conditions for long boards and slalom.

**Accommodation**

There are plenty of cafes and shops in the town of Troon, about 400 yards along the promenade. You can also find accommodation in the town, ranging from budget-friendly options to luxury hotels.

**Water Quality**

The water quality at Troon South Beach is classified as MCSR (Moderate Coastal Recreational Water).

**Suitable for**

Troon South Beach is suitable for everyone except beginners. The beach is exposed to strong winds and waves, so it's best for experienced windsurfers and kitesurfers.

**Tide ID**

Suggested Tide ID: 5404 (based on the nearest port, Troon)

**Additional Information**

Please note that the information provided is subject to change, and it's always a good idea to check the latest conditions and forecasts before heading out. You can also check the UK Hydrographic Office website for up-to-date tide times and information.
