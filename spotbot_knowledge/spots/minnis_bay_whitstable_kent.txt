**Minnis Bay Windsurf Guide**

**Location:** Minnis Bay, Whitstable, Kent

**Suitability:** Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced

**Beach Faces:** N

**Best Wind:** W, NE

**Tidal Range:** Small

**Shelving Rate:** Gradual

**Windsurfing Ratings:**

* Wave: Beginner
* Freestyle: Good
* Speed: Beginner

**Description:** Minnis Bay is a long, family-friendly stretch of beach in the village of Birchington-on-Sea, on the Isle of Thanet. The bay is situated near the town of Whitstable, which is renowned for its windsurfing conditions. The bay provides great flat water for bump and jump sailing, making it an ideal spot for learning and freestyle windsurfing.

**Tide Information:**

* Suggested Tide ID: 0127 (Whitstable)
* High tide: 02:19, 14:32, 15:04, 16:12, 16:54, 17:47, 18:50, 20:51, 21:21, 21:52, 22:28, 23:15
* Low tide: 08:23, 08:52, 09:22, 09:56, 10:37, 11:30, 12:36, 20:51, 21:21, 21:52, 22:28, 23:15

**Launch and Parking:** Launch from the beach to the left of the harbour. Parking is available near the beach, with a short walk to the launch site.

**Features:**

* Flat water for bump and jump sailing
* Gradual shelving rate makes it suitable for beginners
* Good for freestyle windsurfing
* Nearby shop and school for equipment and lessons
* Thriving yacht club with a great social scene

**Considerations:**

* Strong tides and currents can be a challenge
* Steeper beach at high tide can make launching difficult
* Be aware of the wind direction and tidal conditions before launching

**Directions to Minnis Bay:**

* Head towards Margate, about 6 miles from the motorway
* Turn off for Whitstable and look out for a place called Tankerton
* Follow the road which veers sharply right and look for a left turn, this is the parking area
