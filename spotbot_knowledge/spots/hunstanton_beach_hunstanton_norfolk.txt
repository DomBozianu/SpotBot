**Hunstanton Beach Windsurf Guide**

**Location:** Hunstanton Beach, Hunstanton, Norfolk

**Suggested Tide ID:** 5444 (based on King's Lynn, the nearest port)

**Wind Directions:** Hunstanton Beach is suitable for winds from SSW through W to N.

**Beach Characteristics:**

* Flat water windsurfing and kitesurfing location
* Works in a wide range of wind directions
* Can be sailed at all tides
* Flat water develops behind the sandbar at low tide, but will be shallow
* At high tide, there are groynes, but they shouldn't pose too much of a problem

**Best Wind:**

* N, W, SW

**Tidal Range:** Medium

**Shelving Rate:** Slight

**Windsurfing Ratings:**

* Wave: Good
* Freestyle: Good
* Speed: Good
* Beginner: Good

**Directions to Hunstanton Beach:**

* Follow the road along the shore to reach the beach

**Facilities:**

* Sailing schools
* Caravan sites
* Extensive public amenities

**Safety:**

* The beach has a concrete stepped wall to protect against wave overtopping
* Regular re-cycling operations are undertaken to counteract the southerly longshore drift

**Tips and Tricks:**

* Be aware of the shallow water behind the sandbar at low tide
* Watch out for groynes at high tide
* Take advantage of the flat water and good wind conditions to improve your windsurfing skills

**Insurance:**

* Don't forget to have 3rd party cover as a club rule

**Club Kit:**

* The club has a number of boards and sails for members to use free of charge
* Please ensure that the kit is washed after use and any damage reported so we can make repairs

**More Advanced Sailing:**

* For more advanced sailors, there is a great Astro turf rigging area with wash down facilities
* Join the WhatsApp group to let others know where and when you're sailing when it's windy
