**Hayling Island Windsurfing Guide**

Located in Hampshire, England, Hayling Island is a premier destination for windsurfers, offering a multitude of sailing conditions and exciting experiences. The island boasts several beaches, each with its unique characteristics, making it an ideal spot for windsurfers of all levels.

**Best Beaches for Windsurfing:**

1. **West Beach:** This is the most popular windsurfing spot on Hayling Island, offering a multitude of sailing conditions. It's perfect for flatwater blasting or freestyle in strong westerly winds at low tide. In south-west or southerly winds, the waves wrap around the sandbar, providing fun jumping and easy wave riding.
2. **Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC):** Located on the east side of Hayling Island, HISC is a superb spot for windsurfers. The beach is gated, and access is restricted to members or guests with a pass. The club offers a sandy beach, changing rooms, bars, and restaurants. The tide creates a unique opportunity for windsurfers to practice without tacking, making it an ideal spot for intermediates and above.
3. **Esso Beach:** Located next to the Oyster Beds in Langstone Harbour, Esso Beach is a high-tide spot that's mellower than sailing off the seafront. The shingle bank blocks most of the chop, making the water flatter. It's a haven for beginners, intermediates, and freestylers.
4. **West Town Beach:** This beach is perfect in SW winds, with a wide variety of conditions. The top part of the beach is shingle, and the groynes coupled with a noticeable shore break can make launching difficult at high tide. At all other times, when the sand is exposed, it's a sailing area with a wide variety of conditions.

**Ideal Wind Directions:**

* West Beach: SW, W
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): W, N, NE, E, SE, S
* Esso Beach: SW, W
* West Town Beach: SW

**Ideal Wind Speed:**

* West Beach: 15-25 knots
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): 15-25 knots
* Esso Beach: 15-25 knots
* West Town Beach: 15-25 knots

**Tide:**

* West Beach: Best at Low Tide (+/- 2 hrs)
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): Best at Low Tide (+/- 2 hrs)
* Esso Beach: High Tide
* West Town Beach: Low Tide

**Suggested Tide ID: [4-digits]**

Based on the nearest port, Chichester, the suggested tide ID is:

* West Beach: 4.2 (Low Tide)
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): 4.2 (Low Tide)
* Esso Beach: 4.8 (High Tide)
* West Town Beach: 4.2 (Low Tide)

**Hazards:**

* Strong tidal currents at Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC)
* Submerged groynes at Esso Beach
* Heavy shore dump at high water at Esso Beach
* Busy with boat traffic at all beaches

**Parking and Launching:**

* West Beach: Ample parking along the promenade; launch from the shingle beach
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): Plenty of parking; launch from the sandy beach
* Esso Beach: Parking can be found across the road; launch from the shingle beach
* West Town Beach: Large pay and display car park; windsurfers should park at the far W

**Amenities:**

* West Beach: Toilets, bar/restaurant, water hose
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): Changing rooms, bars, restaurants
* Esso Beach: Toilets, water hose
* West Town Beach: Toilets, bar/restaurant, water hose

**Suitable for:**

* West Beach: Intermediates and above
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): Intermediates and above
* Esso Beach: Beginners, intermediates, and freestylers
* West Town Beach: Intermediates and above

**Getting There:**

* West Beach: Head into the centre of West Town and turn right at the roundabout and into the long beach front car park
* Hayling Island Sailing Club (HISC): Head to the east side of Hayling Island and follow the signs to the club
* Esso Beach: Head to Langstone Harbour and follow the signs to the Esso petrol station
* West Town Beach: Head into the centre of West Town and turn right at the roundabout and into the long beach front car park

**Accommodation:**

* West Town: Inns, hotels, and self-catering apartments available

**Local Shops and Lessons:**

* Andy Biggs Windsurfing Shop: Located in West Town, offering the latest equipment and lessons

Hayling Island is a premier destination for windsurfers, offering a multitude of sailing conditions and exciting experiences.
