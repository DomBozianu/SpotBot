**Exmouth Duck Pond Windsurf Guide**

**Location:** Exmouth Duck Pond, Devon

**Best Time:** Summer months (June to September) when the tide is high and the wind is blowing from the NW, W, SW, S, or SE.

**Tide Information:**

* Suggested Tide ID: 0709 (based on the tide times for Exmouth Dock, which is the nearest port)
* High tide: 07:52 (when the water height is approximately 3.69m)
* Low tide: 13:44 (when the water height is approximately 0.41m)

**Wind and Weather:**

* The best wind direction for windsurfing at Exmouth Duck Pond is from the NW, W, SW, S, or SE.
* Be aware of the wind speed and direction, as it can change quickly.
* Check the weather forecast before heading out to ensure safe and enjoyable conditions.

**Safety Guidelines:**

* Always wear a life jacket and follow the safety guidelines provided by the Exmouth Duck Pond website.
* Keep a safe distance from other water users, including swimmers, kayakers, and powerboats.
* Be aware of the moorings and waterski area when in use.
* Do not practice on the grass or launch/land in crowded areas.
* Disable your kite when not in use and keep it secure.

**Launch and Landing:**

* When the tide permits, launch from the sand.
* Keep at least 75m away from all embankments, except when launching.
* Be aware of the Railway line behind the eastern embankment.

**Other Activities:**

* The Exmouth Duck Pond is also used for water-skiing, power-boating, and walking.
* Be respectful of other users and follow the rules and guidelines provided.

**Tips and Tricks:**

* Arrive early to set up and launch before the wind picks up.
* Be prepared for changing wind and weather conditions.
* Take regular breaks to rest and rehydrate.
* Enjoy the beautiful scenery and wildlife of the Exmouth Duck Pond!

**Conclusion:**

Exmouth Duck Pond is a great spot for windsurfing, with consistent winds and a variety of water conditions. Remember to follow the safety guidelines, respect other users, and be prepared for changing weather conditions. Have a great time windsurfing at Exmouth Duck Pond!
